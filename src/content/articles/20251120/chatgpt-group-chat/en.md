On November 14, 2025, OpenAI added a new group chat feature to ChatGPT, launching a pilot in four regions including Japan. This feature evolves ChatGPT from a simple AI assistant into a social tool that helps friends and family collaborate.

## Group Chat Feature Overview

### Pilot Regions

Group chat functionality is currently piloting in four regions:

- **Japan**
- **New Zealand**
- **South Korea**
- **Taiwan**

OpenAI plans to refine the feature based on feedback from these regions before rolling it out globally.

### Basic Functionality

Group chat works similarly to messaging app group chats:

- **Group Creation**: Create group conversations with friends and family
- **ChatGPT Participation**: ChatGPT functions as one of the conversation participants
- **Natural Dialogue**: Participates in conversations like human participants

## Key Features

### Powered by GPT-5.1 Auto

Group chat leverages **GPT-5.1 Auto**:

- **Dynamic Model Selection**: Automatically selects optimal model based on prompt
- **Efficient Processing**: Lightweight models for simple questions, high-performance models for complex tasks
- **Cost Optimization**: Allocates appropriate resources as needed

### Smart Conversation Flow

OpenAI focused on teaching ChatGPT to understand group conversation dynamics:

- **Timing Judgment**: Understands when to speak and when to stay quiet
- **Context Awareness**: Follows conversation flow and contributes at appropriate moments
- **Natural Participation**: Blends naturally into conversations like human participants

### Mention Feature

Participants can summon AI anytime by mentioning "ChatGPT":

```
@ChatGPT Any good Italian restaurants nearby?
```

ChatGPT responds not only when explicitly mentioned but also speaks up spontaneously when judged appropriate based on conversation flow.

## Practical Use Cases

OpenAI proposes various applications for group chat functionality.

### 1. Travel Planning

When planning trips with friends or family, ChatGPT supports:

- **Destination Suggestions**: Recommending destinations based on budget and preferences
- **Itinerary Creation**: Creating itineraries matching schedule and budget
- **Booking Information**: Providing hotel and transportation information
- **Translation Support**: Language support for international travel

### 2. Renovation Projects

When planning home renovations:

- **Idea Provision**: Suggesting designs and layouts
- **Budget Estimation**: Calculating approximate costs
- **Contractor Selection**: Points for selecting contractors and checklists
- **Project Management**: Creating task lists and schedules

### 3. Restaurant Selection

When planning group dining:

- **Restaurant Search**: Suggesting restaurants considering everyone's preferences
- **Menu Verification**: Addressing allergies and dietary restrictions
- **Reservation Support**: Reservation notes and information
- **Access Information**: Location and parking details

### 4. Event Planning

Planning birthday parties or gatherings:

- **Theme Suggestions**: Party themes and concepts
- **Shopping List**: Creating lists of needed items
- **Recipe Suggestions**: Food and dessert ideas
- **Game Suggestions**: Party games and activity proposals

### 5. Study Groups

For student or professional study groups:

- **Study Planning**: Creating effective study schedules
- **Concept Explanation**: Explaining difficult concepts clearly
- **Problem Solving**: Supporting assignments and homework
- **Resource Provision**: Recommending additional learning resources
