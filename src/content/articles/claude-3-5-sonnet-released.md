Anthropic has launched Claude 3.5 Sonnet, featuring significant improvements across various domains while maintaining their commitment to AI safety.

Users report that Claude 3.5 Sonnet is more accurate at understanding and executing detailed instructions, reducing the need for prompt refinement.
