## Gemini Tops Global Search Trends

Google has released its annual "Year in Search" report, revealing the search trends of 2025. The top global trending search this year was "Gemini," Google's AI chatbot.

## AI Chatbots Dominate Search Trends

Multiple AI-related terms appeared on the trending list. Notably, Chinese AI company DeepSeek ranked 6th globally (7th in the US), clearly demonstrating the worldwide surge in interest in AI technology.

### Global Top 10 Trending Searches

1. Gemini
2. India vs England
3. Charlie Kirk
4. FIFA Club World Cup
5. Asia Cup
6. DeepSeek
7. ICC Champions Trophy
8. (Other sports and entertainment topics)

### US Top 10 Trending Searches

In the US, searches like "AI Barbie" and "AI Ghostface" also ranked high, showing how mainstream generative AI tools have become.
