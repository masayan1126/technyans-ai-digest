OpenAI added a new shopping assistant feature called "Shopping Research" to ChatGPT on November 24, 2025, timed for the Black Friday shopping season.

## What is Shopping Research

Shopping Research is a new shopping assistant feature available within ChatGPT. It researches product information across the internet and creates personalized buyer's guides for users.

Key features:
- **Clarifying questions**: Asks questions to understand user needs
- **Deep research**: Extensively searches product pages, reviews, and prices across the internet
- **Quality sources**: References trustworthy review sites and high-fidelity web content
- **Personalized guides**: Generates customizable buyer's guides based on user requirements

## How to Use

Shopping Research can be accessed through:

1. **Automatic activation**: Automatically triggers when you ask shopping-related questions
2. **Manual selection**: Select "shopping research" from the (+) menu in the app or desktop

Supported plans:
- ChatGPT Free
- ChatGPT Go
- ChatGPT Plus
- ChatGPT Pro

For Pro users, Shopping Research is also integrated into "ChatGPT Pulse" (daily curated updates), proactively suggesting personalized buyer's guides based on past conversations.

## Differences from Previous Shopping Features

While OpenAI has offered shopping capabilities in ChatGPT before, Shopping Research provides more advanced research functionality:

- **Similar to Deep Research**: When given a product description, ChatGPT searches the internet to build a guide
- **Comparative shopping**: Analysis comparing different products
- **Deal discovery**: Finding sales and discount codes
- **Similar product suggestions**: Recommending alternatives to specified items

## Transparency and Trust

OpenAI emphasizes reliability in Shopping Research:

- **Organic reviews**: References actual reviews and web content, not advertisements
- **Links to trustworthy sources**: Provides links to review sites for users to verify details
- **Respecting block settings**: Honors websites that block the research function from accessing their sites

Earlier this month, OpenAI CFO Sarah Friar mentioned commerce as a path to profitability:

## For Merchants

OpenAI provides an allowlisting process for merchants who want their products to appear in Shopping Research results.

## Summary

ChatGPT Shopping Research offers a new AI-powered shopping experience:

- **Free access**: Available on Free, Go, Plus, and Pro plans
- **Deep research capabilities**: Extensive internet search functionality
- **Personalization**: Generates guides tailored to user preferences
- **Transparency**: Clearly cites trustworthy sources for user verification

Heading into Black Friday and the holiday season, OpenAI is positioning ChatGPT as a shopping partner while advancing its commercialization strategy.
